Aurora Delouvroy

VISUAL ARTIST

Aurora Delouvroy (BE) is an aspiring artist who studies at Luca School of arts in Ghent (Painting).
Aurora is mainly interested in combining different art disciplines. She experiments with mediums like drawing, painting and photography. Furthermore she makes use of digital media and creates photoshop paintings by recycling her own photographic material.
By doing so she acquired an interest in the mass production of a painting. She stopped viewing the painting as a unique piece.
